【[希望之声2020年11月10日]( "https://www.soundofhope.org/post/441532")】（本台记者韩梅综合报导）美国国务卿[蓬佩奥]( "https://www.soundofhope.org/term/4007")（Mike Pompeo）当地时间11月10日在[里根研究所]( "https://www.soundofhope.org/term/416488")（Ronald Reagan Institute）演讲﹐详述川普政府对华政策时，用了迄今最强的措辞来描述中共政府。  
  
综合媒体消息，[蓬佩奥]( "https://www.soundofhope.org/term/4007")此次演讲的题目是《[美国的承诺]( "https://www.soundofhope.org/term/416491")》。其中他称中共是一个“马列主义怪物”，其统治是“专制的、残暴的、与人类自由背道而驰的”，并明确说，美国将中共视为“当今世界上自由的头号威胁”。  
  
[蓬佩奥]( "https://www.soundofhope.org/term/4007")表示，川普政府已动员各部门﹐运用所有工具﹐应对中共挑战﹐美国需要帮助中国人民获得自由信息﹐[推倒网络防火墙]( "https://www.soundofhope.org/term/416494")：“我们的基本工作是﹐保证中国人民可以获得信息数据﹐所有他们需要知道的﹐这样他们也能分享到我们所珍视的自由。我们要拥有能力﹐让他们能推倒这个禁锢中国的网络防火墙﹐这将让中国人民做出完全不同的决定﹐不同于现在领导人带他们所走的路。”  
  
**演讲视频及原文:**  

[The Promise of America ]( "https://www.state.gov/the-promise-of-america/")

  

The good news is that the free world and sovereign nations are beginning to wake up.  They are now rallying to this cause.  I’ll often hear, we don’t want to pick between the United States and China.  I remind them that that’s not the fight.  The fight is between authoritarianism, barbarism on one side and freedom on the other.  
  
And so we’ve begun to strengthen the institutions that can achieve this objective.  From the Quad, to ASEAN, to NATO, we’ve woken them up to the threat posed by this **Marxist-Leninist monster.**  
  
The new and lasting consensus on the Chinese Communist Party is an historic result of America’s strength and candor, precisely the traits that President Reagan spoke of.  
  
And for us to have the capacity to permit them to tear down this firewall that has been built around China would enable the people of China to make a much different set of decisions than the one that their current leadership **has taken them down** – the path their current leadership is taking them down.
