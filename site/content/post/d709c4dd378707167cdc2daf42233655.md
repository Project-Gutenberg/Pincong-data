在WhereIsPengShuai追问声中毫无存在感的奥委会主席突然以未知的方式和彭帅取得了视讯联系，不知会不会给追问多日的WTA主席同样的机会。据说30多分钟的视讯，但奥委会只公布了一张照片，照片中彭帅露出了中国特色新时代社会主义的独有笑容。尽管自己刚在两个多星期前自曝猛料，却表现出极强的隐私意识。彭帅称自己呆在家里很好很安全，但没有提张高丽会不会再来约球。视讯全程由奥委会中方代表李玲蔚陪同，在融洽的气氛中结束，四人包括中方代表甚至口头约饭帝都再聚。更神奇的是，墙内的所有媒体都不约而同地无视这条大新闻。这次视讯再次验证彭帅党性未泯，奥委会主席也光荣地接过了世卫火炬。  
  
Although the president of the IOC Thomas Bach hadn't spoken a single word in the #WhereIsPengShuai movement, somehow he had the very first video call with the Chinese tennis star today after her recent disappearance, which could fairly confuse the WTA chairman & CEO Steve Simon, the man who is urging proof of Peng Shuai's safety. The IOC hasn't explained how they got the chance or confirmed whether the talk was free and open, considering the Chinese official Li Lingwei also joined the call. However, Thomas Bach and his colleagues only provided a photo instead of a full-length video tape, in which Peng Shuai showed an unbelievably big smile, something usually appears in communist propaganda. The more weird thing is that all Chinese media and social platforms don't report this big news for Chinese audience. In the IOC's statement, Peng Shuai claimed she is well and safe at home and needs privacy ([which is actually secrecy involving a political scandal](https://pincong.rocks/article/id-37054 "https://pincong.rocks/article/id-37054")) despite her lengthy confess and accusation on Weibo. Moreover, the statement doesn't mention Peng Shuai's sexual assault allegation against the former Chinese vice premier Zhang Gaoli at all. The scoop seems to imply that the IOC's Thomas Bach has token the torch from the WHO's Tedros Adhanom's hand or Peng Shuai has already made a deal with the party.
