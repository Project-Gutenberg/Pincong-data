請願已經設好，要求台灣政府提供在台共產黨隨附組織的個人及家庭資料給AIT  
並將上述成員全部禁止移民美國，請大家發起聯署。  
  
英文請願内容如下：  
  
USCIS Policy Manual addressed inadmissibility based on membership in or affiliation with the Communist Party. Any intending immigrant who is a member or affiliate of the Communist Party or subdivision, domestic or foreign, is inadmissible to the United States.  
We believe that this law should apply to political parties or other organizations that are registered in Taiwan and are obviously affiliate to the Chinese Communist Party in terms of funding and policies.  
We require that the personal information concerning the members of the above organizations and their family should be provided to AIT by the Taiwanese government and be prohibited from immigrating to the United States in order to comply with the implementation of relevant U.S. laws.  
  
各位觀眾，美國國會正式認證，貴匪成為跨國犯罪集團（其實是恐怖組織），所以我們主動要求AIT審查這些犯罪集團附屬成員資料，以免他們潛入美國繼續作惡，顯然是幫美國維護國家安全！
