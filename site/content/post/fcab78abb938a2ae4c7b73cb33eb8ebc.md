USA: Antifa protesters rally at BLM plaza, march in DC as election day ends United States, Washington DC  
  
November 4, 2020 at 17:37 GMT +08:00   
  
A large group of Antifa protesters gathered at Black Lives Matter plaza on Tuesday night, as the 2020 election night came to a close on Tuesday. As more people joined the demonstration in the US capital, the protesters lit off fireworks before marching through Washington DC's streets and dispersing near Columbia Heights. The crowd, dressed in all black, wearing helmets and carrying signs, set a few bins on fire and chanted anti-police slogans in front of a Washington DC police station. A protester was reportedly arrested amid tensions with police.
