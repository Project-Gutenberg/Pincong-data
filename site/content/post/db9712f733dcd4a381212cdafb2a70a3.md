关于广东工业大学鲁仁全教授的国家奖材料

广东工业大学鲁仁全教授因多年前全文剽窃同门师弟的论文而被IEEE撤稿，并被禁止向其期刊投稿。

https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/1712567

https://retractionwatch.com/2021/11/12/ieee-retracts-plagiarized-paper-after-retraction-watch-inquiries/

最近鲁仁全教授申请了国家自然科学奖，以下是具体信息：

项目名称：变结构无人自主系统控制理论与应用

代表性论文 1：Observer-Based Fault Detection for Nonlinear Systems With Sensor Fault and Limited Communication Capacity, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, vol. 61, no. 9, pp. 2745-2751.

代表性论文 2：Stability and L2 Synthesis of a Class of Periodic Piecewise Time-Varying Systems, IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, vol. 64, no. 8, pp. 3378-3384.

代表性论文3： Finite-Time State Estimation for Coupled Markovian Neural Networks With Sensor Nonlinearities，IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, vol. 28, no. 3, pp. 630-638.

代表性论文4：Finite-Time Distributed State Estimation Over Sensor Networks With Round-Robin Protocol and Fading Channels, IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, vol. 48, no. 1, pp. 336-345.

代表性论文5：Synchronization on Complex Networks of Networks,IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, vol. 25, no. 11, pp. 2110-2118.

代表性论文1是关于非线性系统的，代表性论文2是关于周期分段时变系统的，代表性论文3是关于马尔科夫神经网络的，代表性论文4是关于传感器网络的，代表性论文5是关于复杂网络的。这五篇论文不仅研究了五类不同的系统，而且均与"变结构无人自主系统"无关。
