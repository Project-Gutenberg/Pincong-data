此后，这种计划层出不穷，但被[最高领导人](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%9C%80%E9%AB%98%E9%A2%86%E5%AF%BC%E4%BA%BA&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2488560679%7D)当作国家发展战略的只有有限几份。比如，普京上台伊始，他的经济发展和贸易部长[格尔曼 · 格列夫](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%A0%BC%E5%B0%94%E6%9B%BC%C2%B7%E6%A0%BC%E5%88%97%E5%A4%AB&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2488560679%7D)就组织专家制定了一份《[2010 年前俄罗斯发展战略](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=2010%E5%B9%B4%E5%89%8D%E4%BF%84%E7%BD%97%E6%96%AF%E5%8F%91%E5%B1%95%E6%88%98%E7%95%A5&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2439430156%7D)》。2008 年梅德韦杰夫上台后，其首席智囊、[国家发展学院](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%9B%BD%E5%AE%B6%E5%8F%91%E5%B1%95%E5%AD%A6%E9%99%A2&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2488560679%7D)院长[尤尔根斯](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%B0%A4%E5%B0%94%E6%A0%B9%E6%96%AF&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2439430156%7D)也制定了一份 2012 年前俄罗斯战略发展纲要。……（后略）
