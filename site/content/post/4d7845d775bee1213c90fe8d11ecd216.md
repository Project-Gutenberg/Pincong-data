近期，上海有地区在整理分发爱心捐赠的农产品时，因处置不妥当、分拣不及时，导致部分农产品出现腐坏。但相关地区没有准确说明，一度引发外界对捐赠地的误解。对此，理应实事求是，该澄清的澄清、该担责的担责、该改进的改进。这既是对接受物资的市民负责，也是对捐赠物资的兄弟省市人民负责。

除了要对上述 “免费物资” 建立全链条的管理制度，“市场力量”提供的物资同样要从源头把关。

其一，一些明显违法的 “市场行为”，理应严加监管。

例如，有市民反映，部分 “团购” 或“保供企业”存在 “打闷包”“价格虚高”“以次充好” 的情况。这并不符合市场规律，而是市场机制运行缺陷造成的。这些问题，本身就属于市场监管的范畴，有对应的法律法规，应当加快查处、通报。

同时，主管部门和监管部门要更进一步，针对相关企业的保供资质从何而来、取得资质后是否合法合规地供应物资，跟踪管理。对商务、市场监管等部门来说，保供工作应当充分激活调动市场力量，但这不是简单地发证发照，不是 “放任不管”，而是要切实承担起审核、监督的责任。

其二，“市场力量” 是充分竞争、自由选择的力量，应当叫停那些涉嫌垄断的 “伪市场行为”。

目前，官方承认的、具有保供资质的 “市场力量” 已有很多，但有市民反映，部分社区、基层机关等以种种理由，指定供应渠道，但相关渠道提供的商品在品质、价格上均存在瑕疵，不能让市民满意。这个时候，监管部门和纪检部门应当站出来，查一查是否有“黑幕”、是否有“[渎职](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%B8%8E%E8%81%8C&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2452760546%7D)”。只有明明白白、充分竞争的市场，才是居民期待的供应力量。

非常时期的物资供应，直接关系到百姓生活，直接影响这场疫情防控攻坚战的成败。目前，对于物资保障中出现的问题，上海市场监管、公安、检察院、纪委监委等部门都已发声，表示将从快严查。更重要的是，包括商务部门、基层社区等都更多方面也要形成共识——“免费物资”不是 “免责物资”，利用“市场力量” 不是“放任不管”，应当从源头着手、从采购把关，畅通物资供应、强化品质管理，切实维护群众的切身利益。

**方舱医院是什么？**
------------

方舱医院是一种方便搭建的移动医院。它属于野战机动医疗系统的一种，由若干可移动的方舱组合而成，具有紧急收治、外科处置、临床检验等功能。

一旦发生自然灾害或[突发公共卫生事件](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%AA%81%E5%8F%91%E5%85%AC%E5%85%B1%E5%8D%AB%E7%94%9F%E4%BA%8B%E4%BB%B6&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2425354702%7D)，方舱可以在第一时间赶赴现场，原地展开为一所二级甲等医院。

_最早的[医疗方舱](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%8C%BB%E7%96%97%E6%96%B9%E8%88%B1&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2425354702%7D)出现在 20 世纪 50 年代。美军将研制出的军事装备运输方舱，逐渐扩展成为医疗方舱形式，并于 20 世纪 60 年代，率先将其投入越南战场，这就是野战医院方舱化的开始。20 世纪 70 年代，英、德、法等国研制出了各种形式的方舱医院，比如拖车或半拖车式的组合单元；自 20 世纪 90 年代起，不同规模和形式的方舱医院开始出现在各国部队的常规配备中。_

EDITORIAL

Wei-jie Guan1,2,3 and Nan-shan Zhong1,∗

**作者：关伟杰、钟南山**

The transmission of severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus-2 (SARS-CoV-2), the culprit pathogen of coron- avirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), has resulted in more than 0.43 billion cases and 6 million deaths globally. **Compared with other existing variants of concern, the Omicron variant is characterized by significantly greater infectivity (Ro ≈ 6), greater tropism of the upper respiratory tract and lower severity of human infections** \[1\]. These characteristics of the Omicron variant are the basis upon which several developed countries are considering reopening.

Respiratory：relating to or affect respiration

Culprit:the source or cause of a problem

Pathogen: microorganism that can cause disease

Variant: a form or version of something

Tropism: the turning of an organism in a certain direction in response to external stimulus

急性严重的呼吸系统疾病病原体 SARS-CoV-2，作为新型冠状病毒病 COVID-19 的原因，已经在全球造成了超过 4.3 亿例感染和超过 6 百万例死亡。**相比其他现存的值得关注的变种，奥密克戎 Omicron 型变种的特点是, 相比其他变种显著更强的传染性（(Ro ≈ 6)、更强烈的针对上呼吸道的向性 \* 和在人类感染者中更低的严重性。**这些奥密克戎变种的特点是几个发展中国家考虑重新开放的基础。

Different policies have been adopted worldwide during the COVID-19 era. The community-based dynamic zeroing policy has played a pivotal role in minimizing the number of laboratory- confirmed cases and deaths in mainland China. The rapid roll- out of vaccinations, as well as herd immunity, have formed the fundamentals of the ‘total opening’ of some developed countries. This, however, does not necessarily justify rapid and total opening in mainland China. Omicron confers a considerably higher mortality risk than seasonal influenza, and would likely result in social instability and the emergence of other novel variants with a greater infectivity.

pivotal：of crucial importance in success

世界上各个国家在新冠期间采取了不同的政策。基于社区的动态清零政策对中国大陆控制确诊和死亡病例数做出了很大的贡献。迅速推出的疫苗和群体免疫形成了一些发达国家 “完全开放” 的基础。但这并不完全证明迅速完全放开在中国也行之有效。奥密克戎带来了显著高于季节性流感的致死率，且很有可能导致社会不稳定和其他高传染性的变种的出现。

The dynamic zeroing policy has been adopted for maintaining effective disease prevention and control. However, China needs to reopen so as to normalize socio-economic development and adapt to global reopening. **Prolonged dynamic zeroing cannot be pursued in the long run. T**here are several recommendations on how China could reopen in an orderly and effective manner.

动态清零政策是为了保持有效的疾病预防和控制。然而，中国需要重新开放来正常化社会经济发展，并接受全球性的开放。**动态清零从长期角度看无法一直持续下去。**以下是几点关于中国可以有序，有效地重新开放的建议。

First, enforcing nationwide vaccination is crucial to safe- guarding herd immunity. As of 28 February 2022, China has vaccinated 87.64% of the whole population with at least two doses of SARS-CoV-2 vaccines, with 40.32% completing the third-dose boosting vaccination. However, the vaccination rate was still markedly lower than 83% in the Chinese population aged >70 years in mainland China and even lower in Hong Kong \[2\]. The full dose vaccination among over 80% of the population was associated with a markedly reduced mortality rate \[2\]. Homologous boosting with a third dose of BioNTech vaccine significantly increased the neutralizing antibody titers against the Omicron variant \[3\]. Two doses of an inactivated SARS-CoV-2 vaccine (CoronaVac, Sinovac Biotech) conferred protection against the Delta variant \[4\], and an analysis of the protective effects of the third dose of homologous CoronaVac

vaccines on the Omicron variant is underway. Furthermore, recent laboratory data have demonstrated that heterologous boosting of the two-dose CoronaVac vaccine with either the BNT162b2 (Comirnaty, Pfizer-BioNTech) vaccine, the recombinant protein subunit vaccine (ZF2001, Zhifei Longcom Bio- pharmaceutical) \[5\] or the adenovirus vaccine (CanSino Bio- logics) could **markedly enhance protection against the Omicron variant.** These findings have lent support to the heterologous boosting strategy intended to increase the rate of protection against variants of concern (particularly the Omicron variant).

Homologous： having the same relation, in position or structure

Titer: the concentration of a solution as determined by titration

Recombinant ：formed by recombination

Subunit：a distinct component of sth

Heterologous：having a different relation

Adenovirus:DNA virus first discovered in adenoid tissue

首先，全国范围内强制接种疫苗对保障群体免疫有关键作用。截止 2022 年 2 月 28 日，中国总人口的 87.64% 接种了至少两针疫苗，40.32% 接种了第三针疫苗。然而，中国大于 70 岁的老年人的接种率显著小于全民接种率 83%，甚至低于香港。而当完全接种率超过 80% 时就对死亡率下降有了显著作用。第三针接种 BioNTech 疫苗作为同源加强针显著提升了针对奥密克戎的中和抗体滴度 \*。两针 CoronaVac, Sinovac Biotech（科兴）的灭活疫苗提供了对德尔塔 Delta 型变种的抵抗力，而第三针接种同源科兴疫苗后对奥密克戎的防护效果正在分析中。再进一步，近期实验室数据表明，两针科兴疫苗后再接种一针异源加强疫苗，无论是 BNT162b2 即辉瑞与 BioNTech 的 comirnaty（疫苗名），或智飞龙科马生物制药的重组亚单位疫苗，或康熙诺生物的腺病毒疫苗，**都可以显著提升对奥密克戎的保护效果。**这些发现对异源疫苗混种的策略提供了支持，以达到提高对各种病毒变种的保护率的目的（尤其是对奥密克戎）。

Second, efforts to reduce the risk of progression to critical illness and death would benefit from the acceleration of research and development of targeted medications and potent neutralizing antibodies. In addition to paxlovid and molnupiravir, the therapeutic effects of medications (e.g. VV116) and neutralizing antibodies (BRII-196 and BRII-198) have provided hope with regard to improving clinical outcomes, although conduct- ing phase three studies within mainland China would be challenging due to limited patient sources. International collaboration should therefore be encouraged.

Potent：having great power

其次，减少重症和死亡的努力将受益于日渐加速的研究进程、靶向药的发展和有效的中和性抗体 \*。除了 paxlovid 与 molnupiravir（特效药名）外，药品（如 VV116）和中和性抗体（BRII-196 和 BRII-198）的疗效也有利于提高治疗效果，然而中国大陆可能因为有限的患者数使三期研究变得有些困难。因此国际合作是应当被鼓励的。

Third, rapid antigen testing should be prioritized in community settings. Antigen testing has been associated with significantly shorter turnaround times and less reliance on medical facilities and personnel compared to nucleic acid testing. This ensures timely identification of the source patient, on which basis population-based screening with viral nucleic acid testing can be reserved for close contacts (particularly the imported cases).

Antigen：foreign substance which includes immune responses

Screening：the evaluation of sth as a part of survey

再次，快速抗原检测应该是社区的优先选择。抗原检测相比核酸检测的突出特点是更短的测试时间和更轻的对医疗设施和人员的依赖度。这保证了对源患者的及时检测，也可以让核酸检测针对性地被部署在密接筛查中。（尤其是[输入性病例](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E8%BE%93%E5%85%A5%E6%80%A7%E7%97%85%E4%BE%8B&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2454057371%7D)的密接）

Fourth, we should strengthen longitudinal follow-up investigations of infected cases during the latency and convalescent periods. In-depth analysis of the infectivity of imported cases (including those showing re-positive test results) would inform the policy maker with regard to the minimal duration of qua antine and the management of re-positive cases after hospital discharge.

Longitudinal：involving information about an individual over a period of time

Latency:the state of existing but not yet being developed or manifest

Convalescent: recovering from illness

再次，我们应该加强时间角度上对感染病例在未被管控时和康复期的调查。深度分析输入病例的传染情况（包括那些复阳的患者），可以使政策制定者获得最短隔离时间和管理出院复阳患者的信息。

Finally, **performing pilot investigations in certain designated cities or regions**, as well as adjusting the policy according to the epidemic characteristics of imported cases, will be equally vital for verifying the outcomes of the transition towards safe and or- derly social reopening in China.

最后，**尝试在制定城市或地区的试点调查，**以及根据输入病例的传染病特点修正政策，将对证实在中国实行安全有序的社会重新放开政策起到同样关键的作用。
