![](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A//pic3.zhimg.com/v2-bc1aa71685360166eb6e6c26c8df4c74_r.jpg%3Fsource%3D1940ef5c)

最后再说此事是否合法。根据知名媒体的报道，有专业律师质疑此事不符合《[商业特许经营管理条例](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%95%86%E4%B8%9A%E7%89%B9%E8%AE%B8%E7%BB%8F%E8%90%A5%E7%AE%A1%E7%90%86%E6%9D%A1%E4%BE%8B&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2577209373%7D)》的适用主体，**明显违法，属于 “权力变现”**：

![](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A//picx.zhimg.com/v2-14af2c44e162d320e2bcba64cb0165a0_r.jpg%3Fsource%3D1940ef5c)

![](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A//pic1.zhimg.com/v2-52ee99d8704943f139beace6f855ac9c_r.jpg%3Fsource%3D1940ef5c)

事实上，“**商业**特许经营” 和 “**政府**特许经营” 是两回事。不知道这位律师是否了解还有 “[政府特许经营权](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C%E7%89%B9%E8%AE%B8%E7%BB%8F%E8%90%A5%E6%9D%83&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2577209373%7D)”。各地政府都有 “政府特许经营权管理办法”，以成都市政府为例，其《[政府特许经营权管理办法规定](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%94%BF%E5%BA%9C%E7%89%B9%E8%AE%B8%E7%BB%8F%E8%90%A5%E6%9D%83%E7%AE%A1%E7%90%86%E5%8A%9E%E6%B3%95%E8%A7%84%E5%AE%9A&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2577209373%7D)》：

_下列直接关系公共利益、涉及公共资源配置和有限自然资源开发利用的项目，可以实施[特许经营](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%89%B9%E8%AE%B8%E7%BB%8F%E8%90%A5&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2577209373%7D)：（一）城市供水、供气、供热；（二）[污水处理](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%B1%A1%E6%B0%B4%E5%A4%84%E7%90%86&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2577209373%7D)、垃圾处理；（三）城市轨道交通和其他公共交通；（四）法律、法规、规章规定的其他项目。_

涉及到一个三线城市十万人的吃饭问题，应该属于 “公共利益”；办法还规定，BOT 和 OT 模式最长经营期限为 30 年，提供公共服务的最长[经营期限](https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%BB%8F%E8%90%A5%E6%9C%9F%E9%99%90&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2577209373%7D)为 8 年：

![](https://images.weserv.nl/?url=https%3A//picx.zhimg.com/v2-98266c9ebbddc9347fd49d74981c73ab_r.jpg%3Fsource%3D1940ef5c)
