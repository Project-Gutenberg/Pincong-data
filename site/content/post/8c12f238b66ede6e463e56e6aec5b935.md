My message to the Chinese government is free Tibet. Tibet belongs to Tibetans.  
我要对中国政府讲的话就是还西藏自由。西藏属于西藏人民。  
  
I'm here to add my voice and speak out about what‘s happening in Tibet.  
我在此为西藏的境况发声。  
  
Under the Chinese government's brutal rule, Tibetan people's basic rights and freedoms are nonexistent.  
在中国政府的野蛮统治之下，西藏人民的基本权利和自由荡然无存。  
  
They are not allowed to study and learn their language and culture freely.  
中国政府不允许他们自由地学习自己的语言文化。  
  
They are not allowed to travel freely.  
中国政府不允许他们自由迁徙。  
  
They are not allowed to access information freely.  
不允许他们自由地获取信息。  
  
The Tibetan people are not even allowed to worship freely.  
甚至不允许西藏人民自由地进行宗教礼拜。  
  
For more than seventy years, Tibetan monks, nuns, intellectuals, writers, poets, community leaders, activists, and many more  
在过去七十多年里，西藏僧侣、尼姑、知识分子、作家、诗人、社区首领、活动家和其他很多人  
  
have been detained, sent to political reeducation classes, subjected to torture, lengthy interrogations, and even been executed  
他们被拘禁、被送进政治再教育课堂、接受虐待、超时审讯、甚至被处死  
  
simply for excising the freedom that you and I take for granted.  
只因为他们践行了在你我眼中理所当然的自由。  
  
There have been more than five thousand political prisoners in Tibet.  
在西藏，有超过五千名政治犯。  
  
For last twenty five years, five thousand!  
就在过去25年里，逮捕了五千名之多！  
  
Did you know that simply owning a photo of the Dalai lama in Tibet is grounds for arrest.  
你知道吗？仅是拥有一张达赖喇嘛的照片就能让你吃牢饭。  
  
Even flying the Tibetan national flag could get you arrested.  
甚至挥舞西藏国旗也能让你坐牢。  
  
I say, shame on the the Chinese government.  
我只能说，无耻的中国政府。  
  
The Chinese dictatorship is erasing Tibetan identity and culture.  
中国的独裁统治正在抹除藏人的身份与文化。  
  
The cultural genocide in Tibet is so stifling that more than one hundred and fifty Tibetan people have burnt themselves alive,  
对西藏文化的屠杀是如此令人窒息，已有超过150名藏人自焚，  
  
hoping that such a horrific act of sacrifice will raise awareness and attention towards the ongoing abuses within Tibet.  
寄希望于用这种可怕的牺牲来唤起对在西藏正发生的暴政的觉醒与关注。  
  
After learning all of this, I cannot stay silent. I stand with my Tibetan brothers and sisters and I support their calls for freedom.  
了解到这些以后，我无法继续沉默。我与我西藏的兄弟姐妹同在，我支持他们对自由的呼吁。  
  
The communist ideology of China has been around for only about one hundred years, but buddhist civilization, ideology and philosophy have been around for thousands of years.  
中国的共产主义意识形态仅有100年的历史，而佛教文明、意识形态、哲学已有几千年的历史。  
  
Only the Tibetan people should decide the future of Tibet. One hundred percent.  
只有西藏人民有权决定西藏的未来，这毫无疑问。  
  
Tibet belongs to Tibetan people. And I hope and believe Tibet will achieve independence.  
西藏属于西藏人民。而我希望并相信西藏会实现独立。  
  
Brutal dictator of China Xi Jinping, I have a message for you and your henchman.  
中国野蛮的独裁者习近平，我有话对你和你的走狗讲。  
  
I will say again, again, and again, loud and clear. I hope you hear me,  
我会持续发声，洪亮清晰。我希望你能听到。  
  
Free Tibetans. Free Tibetans. Free Tibetans.  
还藏人自由。还藏人自由。还藏人自由。  
  
  
（埃内斯·坎特近日穿着印有雪山狮子旗和“Free Tibet”字样的球鞋比赛，他自己也正受到他祖国土耳其政府的通缉，在美国政治避难。）
