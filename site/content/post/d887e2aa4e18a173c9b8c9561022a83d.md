Hours before she boarded an April 28 Cathay Pacific flight to the United States, the respected doctor who specialized in virology and immunology at the [Hong Kong School of Public Health]( "https://www.foxnews.com/world/chinas-coronavirus-cases-may-have-been-4-times-reported") had plotted her escape, packing her bag and sneaking past the censors and video cameras on campus.  
  
She had her passport and her purse and was about to leave all of her loved ones behind. If she was caught, she knew she could be thrown in jail -- or, worse, rendered one of the "disappeared."  
  
Yan told Fox News in an exclusive interview that she believes the Chinese government knew about the [novel coronavirus]( "https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/infectious-disease/coronavirus") well before it claimed it did. She says her supervisors, renowned as some of the top experts in the field, also ignored research she was doing at the onset of the pandemic that she believes could have saved lives.
