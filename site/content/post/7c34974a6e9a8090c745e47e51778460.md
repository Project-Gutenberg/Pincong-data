既然川粉都说纽约时报揭露了拜登儿子的丑闻，是最客观中立的新闻平台，那么纽约时报的报道了Nebraska州参议员Ben Sasse对川普的评价（附带Ben的语音）：  
[https://www.nytimes.com/2020/10/15/us/politics/ben-sasse-trump.html]( "https://www.nytimes.com/2020/10/15/us/politics/ben-sasse-trump.html")  
  
[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=olSIq\_XQQos]( "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=olSIq_XQQos")  
  
以下为评价节选：  
Senator Ben Sasse of Nebraska 评价川普道：  
  
I don't think the way he's lead through COVID has been reasonable or responsible, or right  
Ben认为川普对疫情的处理是不合理的，不负责任的，甚至是完全错误的  
  
kisses dictators’ butts  
Ben认为川普拍独裁者的马屁。“具体独裁者本人认为是普京，当然也可能是习和金三或沙特的萨勒曼”  
  
sells out our allies under his leadership  
Ben认为川普出卖盟国  
  
he ignores that the Uighurs are in literal concentration camps in Xinjiang right now. He hasn’t lifted a finger on behalf of the Hong Kongers.  
Ben认为川普无视新疆人和香港人的人权运动  
  
mocks evangelicals behind closed doors  
Ben认为川普在背地里讽刺共和党的忠实保守支持者-基督福音派  
  
the way he treats women  
Ben认为川普歧视女性  
  
spends like a drunken sailor  
Ben认为川普像醉汉一样挥霍  
  
His family has treated the presidency like a business opportunity  
Ben认为川普家族政治把总统位置作为生意经用  
  
He’s flirted with white supremacists   
Ben认为川普跟白人至上种族主义者眉来眼去  
  
I’m now looking at the possibility of a Republican blood bath in the Senate, and that’s why I’ve never been on the Trump train：“It’s why I didn’t agree to be on his re-election committee, and it’s why I’m not campaigning for him.”  
Ben承认本次大选，共和党在参议院要面临血战才会保证多数。就因为以上的原因，Ben重申他没有站在川普一边，也不会支持川普的竞选活动。
